How CFR pricing works: CFR passes 3% to customers by default (3.5% with Next Day Funding), which lowers your own processing cost to 1%. Pass the full 4% (4.5% with NDF) — as this invoice does — and your cost drops to 0%. It's a compliant dual pricing model, legal in all 50 states and compliant with all major card brands — not a surcharge, which is heavily restricted and even illegal in some states.
